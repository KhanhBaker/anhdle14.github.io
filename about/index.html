About me!

Welcome to my blog. It is an honor to introduce myself to you guys.

First of all, my name is Le Anh Duc, i am a Vietnamese citizen (for now). After 12 years of primary education1 in Vietnam, i went to a Japan to study bachelor ’s degree in Social Sciences at Ritsumeikan APU2. My major is Social, Media, and Culture. And here i am writing a Computer Science ’s blog to record what i have learned through out the year.

People will ask why did i major in different things, why waste time?

Why learn two things that are not relevant, and just focus on one thing, and be good at it?

Well, my philosophy is very simple. They are both science. Learning a science is not so different after all.

  • You read books,
  • you read a whole lot more books,
  • you research deeper into a subject,
  • and you write an amazing essay to prove the believes based on facts you gathered while reading more about the subject.

At first, the past me, he always thought Information Technology is just a fun project, you love doing it, but never found the believes to do amazing thing with it. It was like a challenge, like a task to solve and nothing else.

The past me has never seen that as a Science, more like a tool to do thing faster, more efficient.

That ’s why i major in Social Science at APU. With what i learned during the 12 years of education is that, people are scary. Especially, coming from a 3th world country, i do think people need to be changed.

So why they didn’t?

Well that is one small question when i first thought of it. Maybe because of the restriction? limitation?. Or because of ignorant?. Than the more i study the more answers i get. And it is not the small to solve, it was so big that reading all the philosophy books doesn’t help solving it. So at least that is what i was aiming to solve, i found a goal, that is AWESOME.

Life only worth living if one examined it enough to find what one is living for.

  • Another version of Plato famous quote: “The unexamined life is not worth living”.3

So this is where Computer Science come to a rescue. I found the goal, but how do i get there? So a means to an end?. Maybe the means is Computer Sciences.

I rant a lot. But to sum it up. I love reading books, i love learning things i don’t know. I love doing philosophy and computer science. My favorite role model is one of my Professor in university, and the well known Alan Kay4 of Computer Science.
